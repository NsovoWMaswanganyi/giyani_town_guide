Welcome to Giyani


The Heartbeat of Limpopo. The Pride of the Tsonga People.


Nestled along the banks of the Great Letaba River lies Giyani — a vibrant town rich in heritage, hope, and hustle. From the traditional beats of Xibelani dancers to the energy of urban youth, Giyani is where culture and modernity meet.

Whether you're here to learn about our proud Tsonga history, connect with the community, or explore local businesses and opportunities — you're in the right place. This is more than just a town. This is home.
